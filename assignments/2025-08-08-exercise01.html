Exercise sheet 1

 Last modified: 12:03 PM - 9 August, 2025

PDF version

  1. Use truth tables to prove that the following are tautologies:

    1. \(P \to (Q \to P)\)

    2. \((P \to (Q \to R)) \to ((P \to Q) \to [P \to R])\)

    3. \((\neg P \to \neg Q) \to (Q \to P)\)

  2. Show that the first FL axiom, along with modus ponens, allows you to show: \(p, q \vdash p\).

  3. Show that the second FL axiom, along with modus ponens, allows you to show: \(p \to q, q \to r, p \vdash r\)

  4. Show that the third FL axiom, along with modus ponens, allows you to show: \(\neg q \to \neg p, p \vdash q\) (Does this remind you of proving by considering the contrapositive?)
